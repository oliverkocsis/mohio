# Problem

Traditional documentation methods are often technical, dense, and inaccessible to non-engineers, leading to misunderstandings, misaligned objectives, and ultimately, products that fail to meet market needs or internal goals. This disconnect not only hinders the efficiency of development processes but also inflates costs and delays market entry.

# Solution
